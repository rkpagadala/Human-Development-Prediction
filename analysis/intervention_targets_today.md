# Who Needs Intervention Today — 19 Target Countries

*The 20–24 cohort year that captures each level's current dropouts differs by education stage. Someone dropping out of primary today will not appear in a 20–24 cohort until 2035–2040. Someone failing to complete lower secondary today shows up in the 2030 cohort. This table uses the correct cohort year for each level.*

| Education level | Cohort year used | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Lower secondary | **2030** | Kids currently in or approaching lower secondary (age 12–17) show up in the 2030 20–24 cohort |
| Primary | **2035** | Kids currently in primary (age 6–12) show up in the 2035 20–24 cohort |

The 2025 cohort reflects decisions already made — those people are adults. It is useful as a baseline but not as an intervention target.
