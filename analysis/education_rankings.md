# Global Education Achievement Rankings — WCDE v3 (1960–2025)

*Supersedes prior analysis/education_rankings.md (old World Bank-sourced analysis, 175 countries). This version uses WCDE v3 data, 204 countries. Source: wcde/output/rankings.md.*


**204 countries** with complete data. Source: Wittgenstein Centre for Demography and Global Human Capital (WCDE v3).
SSP2 (medium) scenario. Age group 20–24. 2020 and 2025 values are SSP2 projections.

| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Ladder Score** | Primary ranking metric. Weighted mean — college 2.5×, upper-sec 2×, lower-sec 1.5×, primary 1×. |
| **Edu Score** | Simple mean of 4 completion levels. Secondary reference metric. |
| **Gain** | Edu Score 2015 minus Edu Score 1960 |
| **Speed** | First year country crossed 60% primary completion |
| **Sequential Gap** | Primary minus lower-secondary 2015 |
